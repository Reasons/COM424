Point, Line, Plane

Description: Explore your environment with a camera, looking for surprising and intriguing visual examples of the design principles: point, line, and plane.


The point is the bright moon in the distance, it's light shimmering over the lake.

Lines make up and form the body of this 'Strandbeest'.

The perspective from above create large planes at the Water Tower place.
