COM - 424: Document Design


Here is the starting point of the online portfolio for COM 424: Document Design for the Fall 2016 Semester.


In this portfolio, you will find the various exercises that were completed in this class. There are many different principles of design that were explored and I hope that you, the reader, enjoy the process that I went through.

Starting out, we had to trace a letterform, three variants of the letter "A", in Adobe Garamond Pro font.

The second exercise had us modifying the various text properties, such as line spacing, rotation, and scale for a word to help give meaning to that word.

Third, We were tasked to design a layout for a paragraph of text in order to give it meaning.

Fourth, we explore the different types of basic shapes, the point, line, and plane.

Fifth, we look at Symmetry and Asymmetry and it's importance.

Sixth, we look at Scale, how size really does matter.

Seventh, we look at Texture, an important part of objects.

Eighth, we look at Color, and it's importance in making things stick out

Ninth, we take a look at Figure/Ground relationships and how focus can really make a difference.
